Our Origin Story

The Day Everything Changed

In a quiet corner of India, our founder indhu ran a modest store selling handcrafted jute bags. One afternoon, a grandmother walked in. She didn't want one bag—she wanted two hundred.

"I don't have that many," the founder replied. The grandma smiled and said, "Then manufacture them. Why settle for a store when you can empower a village?"

She left without buying a single thing, and without leaving a name. We never saw her again. But her vision remained. We moved from retailing to manufacturing, naming our venture Grandma Jute Bags in her honor.
